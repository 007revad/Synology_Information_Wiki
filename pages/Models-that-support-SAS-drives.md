## Models that support SAS drives

From DSM 7.2.1 synoinfo.conf
```
FS6400
FS3600
FS3400
FS3017
FS2017

HD6500

RS18017xs+
RS18016xs+

SA3610
SA3600
SA3410
SA3400D
SA3400

```

UC models **only** support SAS drives, and use DSMUC instead of DSM
```
UC3400
UC3200
```
